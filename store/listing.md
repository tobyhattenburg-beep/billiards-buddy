## Full description (≤4000 chars)
```
Billiards Buddy is the all-in-one companion for pool and billiards players — find a table, level up your game, and connect with the community.

🎱 FIND POOL NEAR YOU
Locate pool halls, billiard rooms, and bars with tables around you. Live GPS search pulls from Google, Yelp, and OpenStreetMap, with ratings, photos, an interactive map, and one-tap directions.

🎯 SHARPEN YOUR GAME
• Shot Analyzer — point your camera at the table for aiming guidance and optional AI coaching on cut angle, speed, and spin.
• Academy — curated video lessons from beginner to advanced.
• Tips & Strategy — 25 fundamentals, cue-ball control, shot-making, and strategy guides.
• Rules — searchable rules for 8-ball, 9-ball, snooker, and more.

🏆 TRACK & COMPETE
• Ranked profile with skill tiers, win/loss record, and a global leaderboard.
• Scoreboard and tournament brackets to run your own events.
• Shot-clock and speed drills to build a faster, sharper game.

💬 JOIN THE COMMUNITY
Chat with players worldwide, share highlights, and challenge others. Report and block tools keep it friendly.

🛒 GEAR UP
Browse cues, cases, chalk, and accessories across budget, mid, and premium tiers.

Works great installed to your home screen. Billiards Buddy — find tables, run the room.
```
