Billiards Buddy — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Billiards Buddy ("the app") is a billiards companion that helps you find pool venues, learn, track scores, and connect with other players. This policy explains what data the app uses, how, and your choices. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Location

When you open the Venues finder, the app requests your device location to find pool halls and bars with tables near you. Your coordinates are:

  • Used to search nearby venues (via Google Places, Yelp, and OpenStreetMap) and to center the map.
  • Sent to those venue-search providers only as latitude/longitude to return nearby results.
  • Stored on your device (in your browser's local storage) as your last known position, so the app can show venues again without re-prompting. It is not uploaded to us.

You can deny or revoke location permission at any time in your device/browser settings; the app still works, but venue search will be limited.

2. Camera

The Shot Analyzer can use your device camera to overlay aiming guidance on a live view of the table. Camera frames are processed for the analysis feature and, if you request AI coaching, a single still image may be sent to our coaching service to generate advice. Camera access is only active while you are using the Analyzer, and only after you grant permission.

3. Community chat & profile

The Community feature uses Google Firebase (anonymous sign-in and a realtime database). When you post a message or appear on a leaderboard, the following may be stored in Firebase: your chosen display name, your avatar choice, messages you send, and game stats (skill, wins/losses). You are signed in anonymously — we do not require your real name, email, or password. Please do not post personal or sensitive information in chat.

4. Information stored on your device

Your profile, settings, stats, favorite venues, and progress are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

5. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase — anonymous auth + chat/leaderboard data.
  • Google Places & Maps, Yelp, OpenStreetMap/Nominatim — venue search and maps.
  • YouTube — embedded lesson videos.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — optional AI shot coaching (processes a shot image you submit).
  • Amazon & eBay affiliate links — the Pro Shop links to these retailers. If you tap a product, you leave the app and shop on the retailer's site under their own privacy policies; we may earn a commission. We do not share your data with them.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy.

6. Children

Billiards Buddy is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

7. Your choices

  • Deny or revoke location, camera, and notification permissions any time in settings.
  • Clear locally stored data by clearing the app's site data / uninstalling.
  • Request removal of chat content you posted by contacting us.
